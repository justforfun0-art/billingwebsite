Good questions

Answers before you sign up.

Is Pride POS GST compliant?

Yes. Every invoice carries HSN codes and the correct CGST/SGST/IGST split based on the customer's state, supports e-invoice IRN where required, and gives you GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B summary exports every month.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Pride POS runs in your browser on any laptop, tablet or phone. You can optionally install it as a Progressive Web App for a desktop-like experience and offline billing.

Does it really work offline?

Yes. Bills, purchases, expenses and other day-to-day entries are saved locally if the internet drops, and sync automatically when you reconnect — invoice numbers are still allocated correctly so two counters never clash.

How much does it cost?

A flat ₹499 per month per business with unlimited bills, items, customers and outlets. There is a 5-day free trial with no card required. See full pricing →

What happens to my data if I stop?

Your account is paused and we keep your data for 30 days so you can resume. After 30 days the business data is archived. Bill and audit history is retained for 6 years as required by GST law.
