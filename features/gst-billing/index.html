Questions

GST billing FAQ

Does Pride POS generate e-invoice IRN?

Yes — for invoices above the turnover threshold where IRN is mandatory, Pride POS generates and prints the IRN and signed QR code.

Can I file GSTR-1 directly from Pride POS?

Pride POS produces the portal-compatible GSTR-1 JSON and an HSN summary you upload to the GST portal or hand to your CA. A one-click direct file is on the roadmap.

Does it handle inter-state sales correctly?

Yes. The tax split is driven by the customer's place of supply, so inter-state sales automatically use IGST and intra-state use CGST + SGST.
